About Sweet Dreams

For over 15 years, Sweet Dreams Bakery has been creating magical moments through our artisanal cakes and pastries.

Founded by pastry chef Maria Rodriguez in 2008, our bakery combines traditional techniques with modern flavors to create unforgettable desserts. Every cake is handcrafted with love, using only the finest ingredients sourced locally when possible.
